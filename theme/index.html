About OpenROV

OpenROV is an open-source, low-cost underwater robot for exploration and education.
It's also a passionate community of professional and amateur ocean explorers and technologists.

Explore

See what OpenROVers are discovering around the world.
  •  

    100 m/333 feet

    OpenROV is capable to reach 100 meter/333 feet of seawater. More than double the depth of recreational SCUBA divers.

  •  

    OpenSource/OpenHardware

    The source code as well as the hardware design are open and extensible.

  •  

    2 hours battery life

    When driving the ROV in a moderate power setting, you get 2 hours or more battery.

  •  

    Gamepad control

    You can drive the ROV with a gamepad. Exploration hasn't been esier!
